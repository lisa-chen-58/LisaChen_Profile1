Diverse Career Path, Singular Passion

My career trajectory has been diverse, shaped by resilience and adaptability. Graduating from WSU with a Civil Engineering degree during the Great Recession, I transitioned to the restaurant industry out of necessity, where I navigated challenges with tenacity.

However, my passion for continuous learning led me to seize opportunities, networking my way into an accounting internship and pursuing a certificate in accountancy. As the world weathered the storm of COVID-19, my role in Corporate Accounting proved essential, yet I yearned for a field that offered boundless growth.

Driven by the allure of perpetual learning, I embarked on a transformative journey into software engineering, graduating from a rigorous bootcamp. My brief stint as a bootcamp tutor reinforced my love for sharing knowledge, culminating in my current role as a Software Support Engineer at a biotech company, where I thrive in an environment of innovation and discovery.

In my role as a Support Engineer, I use a culmination of the skills I've accumulated over the course of my life to effectively triage incoming software tickets and develop user-friendly tools. Working closely with software, I actively engage in troubleshooting and problem-solving to deliver precise solutions and ensure customer satisfaction.

My tenure in Corporate Accounting not only honed my analysis and reporting expertise but also served as a pivotal transition towards software engineering. While initially seeking stability in accounting, I discovered my true passion lay in crafting tools to streamline processes. This realization propelled me towards software engineering, where I found fulfillment in building solutions to empower users.

Rooted in the vibrant environment of the restaurant industry, I cultivated robust team leadership skills and adept problem-solving abilities amidst the hustle of fast-paced operations. Effective communication and unwavering customer service were at the heart of my tenure.
